## The Brief

S4C needed a new game-show format that could engage the 18 to 45 digital audience on a daily basis. The format had to work natively on digital platforms and build a habit-forming community of players.

## Approach

Tinint licensed the Cwis Bob Dydd format from Code Syntax in the Basque Country and adapted it for a Welsh-speaking audience. The daily quiz format created a routine that players returned to, building a growing community of competitive participants. The production ran through a pilot season in 2022 and a full second season in 2023.

## Outcome

With over 18,000 registered users in 16 months, Cwis Bob Dydd became one of S4C's most successful digital engagement projects. It proved that format licensing from minority-language territories can deliver strong results and established a model for future seasons.
