## The Brief

Traditional broadcast audiences are passive. S4C needed a way to convert viewers into active, returning users and build engagement beyond scheduled programming.

## Approach

Led the development of a high-retention digital product built around short, repeatable daily gameplay. Designed a seasonal campaign model combining prize-led acquisition with long-term engagement loops.

## Outcome

Established a loyal base of daily returning users. The format scaled beyond the app into schools, live events and commercial sponsor integrations, proving the long-term value of digital-first broadcast formats.
