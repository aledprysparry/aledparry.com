## The Human Story

At its heart, Cwis Bob Dydd is about the power of a shared daily ritual. The format originated in the Basque Country, developed by the team at CodeSyntax. S4C trusted my vision and backed me with the initial pilot funding to adapt and reimagine this digital concept for a Welsh-language audience.

Three years later, I am immensely proud to say that Cwis Bob Dydd has embedded itself deeply into modern Welsh popular culture. The loop is beautifully simple: 10 random questions, once a day, and a relentless leaderboard where friends, families and colleagues battle for bragging rights. It has grown into a successful commercial property, attracting sponsorship deals. More importantly, it has changed how traditional broadcasters view digital engagement, proving that if you build a compelling, habitual product, audiences will show up every single day.

## The Breakdown

**Type:** Interactive Digital Gaming Product

**Role:** Executive Producer & Product Lead

**The Challenge:** Localising a foreign digital gaming format and shifting a legacy broadcaster's mindset from traditional linear scheduling to daily, product-led user retention.

**The Solution:** A cloud-scalable quiz platform built for rapid daily traffic spikes, supported by hyper-localised content writing and deep social-sharing mechanics.

**Deliverables:** Live daily web application, localised format architecture, commercial sponsorship frameworks and automated daily data pipelines.

**The Impact:** Moving on from seasonal blocks, S4C has officially transitioned the game into an always-on, 365-day-a-year fixture for 2026, with an eye toward eventually migrating this digital phenomenon onto the linear television screen.
