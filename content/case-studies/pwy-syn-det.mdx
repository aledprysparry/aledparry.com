## The Brief

During lockdown, Hansh needed new content that could be produced within pandemic restrictions. The commission called for a fun, relatable format that would work with remote filming.

## Approach

Tinint developed Pwy Sy'n Det? as a dating format adapted entirely for lockdown conditions. The constraints became a creative feature, with the remote setup adding an extra layer of awkwardness and humour to the dating dynamic.

## Outcome

The series demonstrated Tinint's ability to create engaging formats under severe production constraints, delivering content that felt fresh and relevant to audiences living through the same restrictions.
