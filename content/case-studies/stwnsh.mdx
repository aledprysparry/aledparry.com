## The Brief

S4C and Boom Cymru needed a digital production partner to deliver the full range of digital content for their Stwnsh children's brand. This covered everything from websites and online games to mobile apps and interactive elements for live studio shows.

## Approach

As Creative Director at Cube, the work spanned eight years and covered every aspect of digital production for Stwnsh. This included building and maintaining the Stwnsh website, developing online and mobile games, and creating interactive games that integrated directly into the live studio shows.

## Outcome

The multi-year partnership established Stwnsh as a digitally-engaged children's brand, with Cube delivering consistent, high-quality digital content that evolved alongside changes in technology and children's media consumption habits.
