## The Human Story

The rapid rise of generic AI translation models introduced a quiet threat to minoritised languages: the "close enough" mentality. Every day, automated tools push out content that subtly waters down Welsh with rigid, literal translations or incorrect idioms. I refused to accept that "it will do" was the standard Welsh creators and brands had to live with.

I built Capsiynau.com to champion the nuance of the Welsh language in a digital-first world. The goal was to build a highly accurate, context-aware speech-to-text and captioning tool tailored specifically for Welsh grammar and regional dialects, offered at a price point that grassroots creators, independent producers and small businesses could actually afford. It is about protecting linguistic integrity while removing the friction of modern video workflow.

## The Breakdown

**Type:** Digital Product & Language Technology (SaaS)

**Role:** Founder & Product Lead

**The Challenge:** Overcoming the limitations of global AI models that frequently misinterpret or misspell Welsh audio, while making professional localisation accessible to tight budgets.

**The Solution:** A bespoke localisation platform engineered with specialised Welsh speech-recognition language models. The tool delivers accurate, timed captions and translations, bypassing the stiff literal prose generated by mainstream corporate competitors.

**Deliverables:** High-accuracy automated video captioning engine, interactive browser-based subtitle editor, and localised video export tooling.

**The Impact:** Empowering Welsh creators to instantly maximise their social media reach and meet accessibility standards without compromising the precision and beauty of the language.
