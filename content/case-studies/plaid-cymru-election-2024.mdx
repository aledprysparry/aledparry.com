## The Brief

Political broadcasts often feel formal and disconnected, particularly for younger audiences and social-first viewers.

## Approach

Produced a series of videos using a more personal, behind-the-scenes style. Shifted tone to feel direct, conversational and locally grounded.

## Outcome

Created modular content that worked across broadcast and social platforms, maximising reach and adaptability while improving audience connection.
