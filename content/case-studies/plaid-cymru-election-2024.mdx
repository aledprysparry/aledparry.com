## The Human Story

Political party broadcasts often fall into a predictable trap: they feel overly manufactured, rigidly corporate and disconnected from the fast-moving digital conversations happening online. When we won the open tender to produce Plaid Cymru's 2024 General Election campaign, we set out to challenge that status quo.

Delivered via Tinint, our strategy was to build a dual-threat campaign. Instead of treating social media as an afterthought to a traditional TV broadcast, we flipped the script. We shot and structured the campaign so that the dialogue, tone and pacing felt completely native to social feeds, while comfortably meeting the strict technical guidelines required for national television. In true political fashion, a sudden snap election tore up our comfortable timelines. We shifted overnight into a hyper-compressed, high-stakes sprint, working hand-in-hand with Rhuanedd Richards and the core team at Plaid Cymru to deliver an authentic, grounded and human campaign under national scrutiny.

## The Breakdown

**Type:** Political Media & Campaign Production

**Role:** Producer & Digital Lead (via Tinint)

**The Challenge:** Crafting an authentic political voice within a rigid broadcast format, while navigating a highly compressed, unpredictable snap-election delivery timeline.

**The Solution:** An agile production workflow that captured high-production-value assets with alternative scripting and frame variations designed specifically for social media cut-downs.

**Deliverables:** National TV party political broadcasts, targeted social media video asset packages, and rapid-response campaign materials.

**The Impact:** Cutting through standard political noise with a refreshing, human tone, delivered precisely on time despite the chaotic sprint of a snap-election window.
