## The Human Story

I spent 18 months in constant communication with Jim and Sophie, the husband-and-wife creative powerhouse behind Stormjar, waiting for the exact right project to bring our worlds together. The spark happened when I saw them on the news discussing a project they were developing on Flat Holm Island. When the Media Cymru funding calls opened, we knew we had our perfect collaborative canvas.

Our goal was to smash the financial barriers preventing indie filmmakers from accessing high-end visual effects. Traditional CGI workflows are notoriously expensive, locking grassroots creators out of cinematic worldbuilding. Supported by the management teams at Tinopolis Cymru and Media Cymru, we set out to build a lean, accessible VFX pipeline tailored for low-budget storytelling. We didn't want to just publish a dry technical manual, so we pushed the pipeline to its limits by producing a short narrative film that proves exactly what independent creators can achieve when given the right open-source tools.

## The Breakdown

**Type:** R&D Pipeline Architecture & Short Film Production

**Role:** Product Manager & Workflow Lead

**The Challenge:** Making complex, historically cost-prohibitive cinematic visual effects pipelines accessible to independent and grassroots filmmakers.

**The Solution:** An optimised, efficient CGI workflow that leverages modern production tooling to bypass traditional post-production costs.

**Deliverables:** Open-source VFX/CGI workflow blueprint, asset pipelines, and a fully completed narrative short film as a practical case study.

**The Impact:** Bridging the gap between modern production technology and authentic storytelling. The entire lean pipeline is scheduled to be open-sourced to the filmmaking community this year (2026) alongside the release of the short film.
