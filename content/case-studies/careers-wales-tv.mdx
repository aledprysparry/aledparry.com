## The Brief

Careers Wales needed to find new ways to connect with students across Wales, particularly as in-person events became harder to deliver. They required a pilot that could test the viability of live digital content for careers guidance.

## Approach

Tinint produced a series of professionally delivered live webinars, giving Careers Wales a broadcast-quality digital presence. The pilot tested formats, engagement strategies and technical delivery to establish what worked best for school and HE audiences.

## Outcome

The pilot gave Careers Wales a proven model for digital engagement with students, demonstrating that live webinars could complement and extend their traditional outreach.
