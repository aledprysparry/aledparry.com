## The Brief

S4C commissioned a digital pilot to test how the Unity game engine could be used to deliver original Welsh-language content. The project was part of a broader exploration into interactive digital formats for the broadcaster.

## Approach

Ku was developed as a proof of concept within Unity, exploring how game engine technology could create immersive, interactive experiences that went beyond what traditional web or app development could achieve.

## Outcome

The pilot provided S4C with valuable insight into the creative and technical possibilities of game engine technology for Welsh-language content production.
