## The Brief

Hansh wanted a series that could make politics feel relevant and entertaining for younger Welsh-speaking audiences. The format needed to cut through apathy with humour rather than lectures.

## Approach

Presenter Esyllt Sears delivered sharp satirical takes on current affairs in a short-form format built for digital consumption. Tinint produced the series with a tone that was irreverent but informed, making complex political topics accessible without dumbing them down.

## Outcome

Chi' di clywed? proved that political content can land with younger audiences when the tone is right, giving Hansh a distinctive voice in an area most digital platforms avoid.
