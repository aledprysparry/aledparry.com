## The Brief

Real estate content is typically static and fails to capture attention on social platforms, limiting reach and engagement.

## Approach

Created a gamified format where agents compete to guess listing prices, using reaction-led storytelling and audience participation mechanics to drive engagement.

## Outcome

Shifted the brand's content from passive listings to interactive entertainment. Increased engagement and positioned agents as knowledgeable, relatable local experts.
