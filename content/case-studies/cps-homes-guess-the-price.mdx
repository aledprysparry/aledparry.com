## The Human Story

It is a universal human instinct: whenever people walk or drive past a house with a "For Sale" sign outside, they immediately guess the asking price. I saw this everyday habit as a brilliant foundation for an entertaining, high-engagement format. I originally developed the concept as a proof of concept for a major television broadcaster. However, the modern media landscape means broadcasters increasingly expect creators to pilot and self-fund new formats before they will commission them.

Recognising the format's potential, Cardiff-based estate agency CPS Homes stepped in to back the vision. We turned the idea into a fast-paced YouTube pilot. To pull it off with a lean crew, I built a custom, real-time browser-based graphics application. This app allowed us to trigger on-screen presentation graphics instantly during filming, and it doubled as an automated engine to export perfectly formatted, responsive social media assets on the fly.

If anyone would like a live demo of this real-time graphics technology, please let me know. I am always happy to share the build and chat through the stack.

## The Breakdown

**Type:** Digital Content Pilot & Live Production Tooling

**Role:** Format Creator, Director & Frontend Engineer

**The Challenge:** Overcoming the financial and technical bottlenecks of traditional pilot production by creating bespoke, automated graphics software.

**The Solution:** A dual-purpose project. A highly engaging YouTube property game show format, underpinned by a lightweight, browser-accessible graphics application that replaces expensive traditional broadcast hardware.

**Deliverables:** Complete YouTube pilot episode, real-time presentation graphics app, and a responsive media pipeline for instant social media cut-downs.

**The Impact:** A masterclass in rapid prototyping. We delivered a broadcast-quality entertainment pilot while simultaneously engineering a reusable, highly efficient content production pipeline.
