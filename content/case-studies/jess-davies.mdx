## The Brief

Hansh commissioned a presenter-led factual series that would tackle subjects Welsh-language media rarely explores. The format needed a host confident enough to ask direct questions and create a space where contributors felt comfortable discussing sensitive topics.

## Approach

Jess Davies brought a direct, empathetic presenting style to each episode, opening up conversations around subjects that are often avoided. Tinint kept the production stripped back, letting the content and contributors take centre stage.

## Outcome

The series gave Hansh distinctive factual content that stood out in its digital slate, demonstrating that Welsh-language media can address taboo subjects with maturity and engagement.
