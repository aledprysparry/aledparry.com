## The Brief

S4C and TG4 commissioned a travel series that would celebrate the shared Celtic heritage between Wales and Ireland. The production needed to work across two broadcasters and two languages, with digital-first episodes for S4C and longer linear cuts for TG4.

## Approach

Tinint produced Ein Llwybrau Celtaidd with Ryland Teifi and his daughters Lowri and Cifa at the heart of the series, giving it an intimate, family-driven perspective. The production partnered with celticroutes.info to weave genuine cultural and historical connections into each journey.

## Outcome

The series delivered a cross-border co-production that worked for both digital and linear audiences, strengthening the creative relationship between S4C and TG4 while showcasing Celtic heritage to viewers in both nations.
