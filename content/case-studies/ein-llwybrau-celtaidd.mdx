## The Brief

Aligning the priorities of two broadcasters and six county councils within a single production, while maintaining editorial quality.

## Approach

Delivered a collaborative production model where broadcast content was supported by public sector tourism strategy. Managed a complex stakeholder ecosystem to align creative and strategic goals.

## Outcome

Produced a prime-time broadcast series that also functioned as a national tourism campaign, demonstrating the effectiveness of public-private partnerships in media.
