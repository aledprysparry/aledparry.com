## The Brief

As part of a collaboration between S4C, the Urdd Eisteddfod and Theatr Genedlaethol Cymru, short films were commissioned to develop new Welsh-language drama talent. The brief invited bold, contemporary stories that would challenge audiences.

## Approach

Writer Rhiannon Williams delivered a script that confronted white privilege directly and unflinchingly. Tinint produced the film with a focus on letting the writing speak for itself, keeping the production intimate and the performances raw.

## Outcome

Help(u) won the Drama Medal at the 2021 Urdd Eisteddfod, recognising both the strength of the writing and the quality of the production. It demonstrated that short-form Welsh-language drama can tackle the most important contemporary subjects with confidence.
