## The Brief

Part of the short film collaboration between S4C, the Urdd Eisteddfod and Theatr Genedlaethol Cymru, this commission invited emerging writers to create bold, personal Welsh-language drama.

## Approach

Writer Martha Ifan crafted a script that explored the reality of living with obsessive-compulsive disorder. Tinint produced the film with sensitivity and restraint, ensuring the subject matter was portrayed authentically and without sensationalism.

## Outcome

Dan Orfod was shortlisted for the Drama Medal at the 2021 Urdd Eisteddfod, recognising the quality of the writing and the importance of representing mental health in Welsh-language drama.
