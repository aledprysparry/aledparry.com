## The Brief

Hansh wanted a stripped-back comedy format that could be produced quickly and delivered consistently. The brief called for something raw, opinionated and genuinely funny.

## Approach

Tinint kept Rant deliberately simple. Give someone a topic, point a camera at them, and let them go. The format relied entirely on the energy and personality of the contributors, with minimal production getting in the way of the comedy.

## Outcome

Rant proved that the simplest formats can be the most effective. The low production overhead allowed for a high volume of content, while the unscripted format ensured every episode felt fresh and authentic.
