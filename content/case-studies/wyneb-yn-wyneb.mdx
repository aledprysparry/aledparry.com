## The Brief

Hansh commissioned a format that would create space for direct, honest conversation between people who disagree. The series needed to generate real dialogue rather than performative argument.

## Approach

Tinint produced Wyneb yn Wyneb by carefully pairing contributors with genuinely opposing perspectives and creating an environment where both felt heard. The format stripped away studio gimmicks and focused on the power of two people talking face to face.

## Outcome

The series delivered thought-provoking content that stood out in Hansh's digital slate, proving that structured disagreement can be compelling viewing when produced with care.
