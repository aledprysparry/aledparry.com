## The Brief

Ragdoll Productions wanted a digital experience that captured the magic of their Dipdap animation, where a line drawing comes to life and interacts with a curious character. The app needed to let children become part of that creative process, drawing on screen and watching their creations come alive.

## Approach

Cube developed the Dipdap App as an interactive drawing experience within a game engine. Children could draw freely on the screen while the Dipdap character responded to and interacted with their creations, faithfully recreating the spirit of the TV show in a participatory format.

## Outcome

The app reached top chart positions and sustained strong engagement throughout its three-year active period, proving that brand licensing deals can produce genuinely creative digital products when the interactive design respects the source material.
