## The Brief

Hansh commissioned a street entertainment series that would showcase Welsh-language content in public spaces, creating genuinely surprising moments caught on camera.

## Approach

Tinint produced Hud y Stryd with Joseff Badman performing magic on the streets of towns and cities across Wales. The series relied on genuine reactions from unsuspecting members of the public, giving the content an authentic, unscripted energy.

## Outcome

The series delivered highly shareable content for Hansh's digital platforms, with the magic format translating naturally to short-form video and social media distribution.
