## The Brief

S4C needed a complete digital presence for Cyw, their flagship pre-school children's brand. This wasn't a one-off project — it was an ongoing, evolving digital ecosystem that needed to grow with the brand and its audience over many years, all delivered primarily in Welsh.

## My Role

As Creative Director and Producer at Cube Interactive, I led the design, development, and ongoing evolution of the Cyw digital platform from 2007 to 2014 — websites, apps, games, and interactive content across web and mobile.

## Approach

We built a modular content system that allowed S4C's team to create and publish interactive activities, games, and videos across web and app simultaneously. Everything was Welsh-first by design, not as an afterthought.

The platform evolved from a simple companion website to a full multi-platform ecosystem including dedicated iOS and Android apps, interactive games tied to broadcast content, and educational activities aligned with S4C's children's programming schedule.

## Outcome

The Cyw digital platform ran successfully for over seven years, winning a BAFTA Cymru award and becoming one of the most important Welsh-language digital resources for pre-school children. It demonstrated that high-quality Welsh-language digital content could compete with English-language equivalents and helped establish Cube as a leader in bilingual digital production.
