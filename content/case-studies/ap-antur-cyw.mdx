## The Brief

S4C commissioned a free app for their Cyw pre-school brand, designed to extend the channel's reach into the digital space. It needed to be safe, engaging and entirely Welsh-language, giving young children a playful experience with familiar Cyw characters.

## Approach

Tinint developed Ap Antur Cyw as an interactive adventure, using the Cyw characters children already knew and loved. The app was designed for pre-school usability with large touch targets, simple navigation and bright, engaging visuals.

## Outcome

The app provided S4C with a strong digital extension of the Cyw brand, giving families a free, high-quality Welsh-language app for their youngest children.
