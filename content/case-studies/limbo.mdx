## The Brief

S4C commissioned an original scripted comedy series for Hansh's digital platform. The series needed to work in a mid-form digital format while delivering the production values and storytelling depth of traditional broadcast comedy.

## Approach

Tinint produced LIMBO as five fifteen-minute episodes, a format that demanded tight writing and efficient storytelling. Every scene had to earn its place, with the comedy driven by character and situation rather than padding.

## Outcome

LIMBO demonstrated that Welsh-language scripted comedy can thrive in digital-first formats, giving S4C a template for mid-form commissioning on the Hansh platform.
