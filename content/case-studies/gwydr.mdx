## The Brief

Part of the short film collaboration between S4C, the Urdd Eisteddfod and Theatr Genedlaethol Cymru, this commission gave emerging Welsh-language writers an opportunity to create bold, personal drama for screen.

## Approach

Writer Delyth Evans used the metaphor of glass to explore the invisible barriers women encounter in everyday life. Tinint produced the film with a focus on visual storytelling, letting the central metaphor carry the weight of the narrative.

## Outcome

Gwydr was shortlisted for the Drama Medal at the 2021 Urdd Eisteddfod, recognising the script's power and the quality of production. It added to a strong slate of short films that demonstrated the depth of emerging Welsh-language drama talent.
