## The Human Story

Nodiadau started as a question I kept hitting at work: what would a dictation app look like if it actually understood Welsh? Not bolted on, not translated from English, but treated as a first-class language with its own grammar, dialect range and conversational rhythm. The early prototype answered that, and the way people started using it shifted the whole product idea.

It quickly evolved from a technical experiment into a robust daily productivity engine for bilingual workspaces. It moves past simple dictation; Nodiadau acts as an active administrative assistant for teams operating in English and Welsh. By accurately capturing raw, conversational speech, it untangles messy brain dumps into beautifully summarised, actionable project assets. It bridges the linguistic divide in real time, letting teams speak naturally while ensuring nothing gets lost in translation.

## The Breakdown

**Type:** AI Productivity Application (Mobile/Web)

**Role:** Founder & Product Lead

**The Challenge:** Showcasing an API via a practical, high-value consumer application that addresses the workflow friction faced by bilingual creative and corporate teams.

**The Solution:** A smart dictation app that handles fluid language switching, voice note transcription and AI-driven post-processing designed specifically for Welsh-English collaborative workflows.

**Deliverables:** Cross-platform web/mobile dictation app, automated bilingual summarisation modules, dynamic to-do list compilers, and an automated email authoring assistant.

**The Impact:** Transformed a basic API demonstration into a crucial business tool that saves hours of administrative time, allowing bilingual teams to brainstorm freely while the app structures the output.
